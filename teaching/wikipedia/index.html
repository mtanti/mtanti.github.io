Improving the Maltese Language Wikipedia

Description:

The Maltese language Wikipedia website contains around 5 000 articles, making it one of the smallest languages in Wikipedia. These lectures are for those who want our Wikipedia to grow. You will briefly learn how to edit Wikipedia articles and then be given a task to help grow and improve the Maltese Wikipedia, with support from the Wikimedia Community Malta.

This is a Degree Plus study unit given at the University of Malta.
